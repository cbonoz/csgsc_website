Tea Time (TT)

Tea Time is a weekly gathering of CS graduate students, faculty, and staff. Come get some free food, fancy tea and coffee, and talk to your fellow students and faculty!

Tea Time occurs most Wednesdays during the school year at 3:30-4:30pm in Boelter Hall 4760. It is organized and host by the CSGSC; except for sponsored events, Tea Time is funded by the CS department.

Volunteers are needed!!! If you wish to volunteer, please email Katherine Wang at k8wang@cs.ucla.edu.

Corporate sponsors please contact Bill Goodin for more information.
