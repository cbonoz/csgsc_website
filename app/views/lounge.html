Lounge

Boelter Commons is a lounge area for all computer science graduate students. We go there to collaborate on group work or relax after a long day of research. Some features of the lounge include:

  • Group work tables
  • Plush desk chairs
  • Comfortable couches
  • 63" presentation monitor
  • Tornado© foosball table
  • Magazines
  • Whiteboard wall

The lounge is located in 3256-S Boelter Hall, just north of the Graduate Student Computer Lab.

If you are a CS graduate student, you should already have swipe card access to this room.

Please keep the lounge clear.
